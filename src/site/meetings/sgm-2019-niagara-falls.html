What is the Spring General Meeting?

The Spring General Meeting (SGM) is an opportunity for Canadian medical students to get together and discuss important issues pertaining to medical education, advocacy, global health, wellness, and more. Join the conversation, get involved — we want to hear from you!


Registration:
How can you register? You can do it online! (Deadline: 11:59 PM EST on March 12th, 2019)

Registration is NOW OPEN. Registration costs are $125.00 and you must also register for CCME (Student Rate of $50.00).

You can register online via SurveyMonkey HERE. Please follow all instructions carefully.
NOTE: If you are a self-funded registrant, please pay ONLINE prior to registering on SurveyMonkey to ensure we have your funds.

Please direct any registration inquiries to office@cfms.org. 

You will be able to pay your fees online very soon. We are working on getting our online payment portal up and running.


Travel Funding:
