Resources Supporting Advocacy

Advocacy Toolkits

Canadan Medical Association: Guide to Political Action

https://www.cma.ca/Assets/assets-library/document/en/advocacy/MD-MP-SkillsGuidebook-e.pdf

  • Introductory, 20-page guide to Canadian government and physician advocacy
  • Describes how bills become law, glossary of terms, template conversations with Members of Parliament

Canadian Medical Association: MD-MP Contact Program

https://www.cma.ca/Assets/assets-library/document/en/advocacy/MD-MP-Handbook-e.pdf

  • Introductory, 16-page guide to effective conversations with Members of Parliament, building relationships, and letter-writing

Financial Resources

Canadian Federation of Medical Students: Student Initiative Grants

http://www.cfms.org/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=149&Itemid=1026&lang=en

  • Funding available for projects that enhance medical education, the health care system, or the local community

Alberta Medical Association: Emerging Leaders in Health Promotion Grant Program

https://www.albertadoctors.org/about/awards/health-promo-grant

  • Grant program for the health advocacy initiatives of medical students and residents

Ontario Medical Students Association: Innovator Grants

http://www.omsa.ca/

  • Funding available for projects that enhance Ontario medical education, the provincial health care system, or the community

Advocacy Awards

Canadian Medical Association: Sir Charles Tupper Award for Political Action

https://www.cma.ca/En/Pages/charles-tupper-award.aspx

  • The Sir Charles Tupper Award is presented at General Council to a member of the MD-MP Contact Program or other CMA member who has demonstrated leadership, commitment, and dedication in advancing the goals and policies of the CMA through grassroots advocacy

Canadian Medical Association: Awards for Young Leaders

https://www.cma.ca/En/Pages/awards-young-leaders.aspx

  • Awarded for exemplary commitment and leadership in one of the following domains: political, clinical, education, research and community service

Doctors of British Columbia: Changemaker Award

https://www.doctorsofbc.ca/resource-centre/awards-scholarships/changemaker-medical-resident-and-medical-student-advocate-awards

  • The Changemaker Award recognizes medical residents and students who have demonstrated exemplary leadership and dedication to the cause of advancing the policies, views, and goals of Doctors of BC, or of a medical resident / student organization in BC, through grassroots advocacy efforts
