What is the Spring General Meeting?

The Spring General Meeting (SGM) is an opportunity for Canadian medical students to get together and discuss important issues pertaining to medical education, advocacy, global health, wellness, and more. Join the conversation, get involved — we want to hear from you!

Travel Funding

Never been to a CFMS meeting? Interested in getting involved? Get your registration and travel costs covered by the CFMS by applying for Travel Funding! Each year the CFMS and MD Financial Management allocates funds to bring medical students to our annual meetings. It's a great way to learn more about what the CFMS does, get involved, and meet other medical students from across the country.

Call for Nominations: CFMS President-Elect

The election will take place at the CFMS Spring General Meeting (SGM) on Saturday April 28, 2018 in Halifax, NS. Votes will be cast by medical student society representatives and members of the Federation’s executive, as detailed in the organization’s bylaws. For more information on the nomination process, click here.

Call for Resolutions

Medical societies and meeting delegates are invited to put forward resolutions for discussion and debate at the CFMS Spring General Meeting during the Members Resolutions Session (tentatively scheduled for Saturday, April 28th, 2018). Resolutions should reflect an understanding of ongoing and past work, and resolutions must pertain to new work of the Federation. Members are encouraged to review resolutions submitted at the previous meeting, available on the CFMS website under the SGM 2018 meeting documents section.

Resolutions must be received by Jessica Bryce, CFMS SGM Chair, by e-mail at resolutions@cfms.org on or before Friday, April 13th, 2018 at 19:00 ADT to be pre-circulated to SGM delegates via our website. Additional written resolutions may be tabled if emailed to the SGM Chair by Friday, April 27th, 2018 at 12:00 noon ADT.
