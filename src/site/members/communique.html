The CFMS Communiques are a great way to stay up to date with the latest CFMS news, external updates, and opportunities for involvement! They are sent biweekly throughout the academic year (Sept.-Apr.) and less frequently throughout the summer months. For more information, please contact vpcommunications@cfms.org.

CFMS Communiques are now distributed via the online marketing service MailChimp. In addition to CFMS Reps at each school, the Communiques are made available to all interested medical students.
