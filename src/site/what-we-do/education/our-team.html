Our Team

The CFMS Education Committee serves the educational needs of Canada’s medical students alongside the CFMS Director of Education.

If you have concerns, ideas, or projects you’d like to share please contact both the Director of Education and the pertinent File Lead.

Director of Education

  • Maylynn Ding
  • Michael G. DeGroote School of Medicine - McMaster University

  • Maylynn is responsible for directing the representation, analysis and policy of the CFMS on educational issues. She represents the Federation at such forums where concerns of a primarily educational nature are discussed (listed below). Maylynn also undertakes initiatives to enhance the educational experience of members of the Federation.

  • If you have concerns or ideas you would like raised at the below listed organizations, please don’t hesitate to contact the Director of Education: Association of Faculties of Medicine of Canada , Royal College of Physicians & Surgeons of Canada ,Federation of Medical Regulatory Colleges of Canada , and Canadian Residency Matching Service . In addition, the Director of Education oversees ex officio members who represent the CFMS to many external organizations as listed below.

  • education@cfms.org

Education Committee

Education Attaché

  • Rishi Sharma

Rishi Sharma is one of the Ontario Regional Directors and attaché to the Education portfolio. As attaché, he provides support to all aspects of the Education Portfolio and steps in to fulfill the role of Director of Education when Maylynn is unable to. 

National Officer of Education

  • Nathan Rider

The National Officer of Education (NOE) acts as the manager of the Education Committee and the Vice-President Academic Roundtable. The NOE also liaises with education-related projects undertaken by the CFMS general membership.

National Officer of Research Sr.

  • Raechelle Gibson

The National Officer of Research Sr. (NOR) oversees all aspects of the Research Committee and is responsible for leading the research initiatives of the CFMS. The NOR Sr. oversees survey design, REB approvals, data analysis, and building the research aspect of the CFMS.

National Officer of Research Jr.

  • Lia Huo

The National Officer of Research Jr. (NOR) assists the NOR Sr. with all aspects of the Research Committee. The NOR Jr. fills in for the NOR Sr. when he/she is not available. The NOR Jr. assists with survey design, REB approvals, data analysis, and building the research aspect of the CFMS.

Health Human Resources

  • Ben Chen

The Health Human Resources (HHR) file works on improving the quality and transparency of HHR data available to medical students in the career planning process.

Matchbook

  • Rafael Zaki

The Matchbook file oversees the creation and organization of the annual CFMS Matchbook to help support students entering the CaRMS match process.

Competency-Based Medical Education

  • Silvio Ndoja
  • Vivesh Patel

The Competency-Based Medical Education (CBME) file is working on a literature review and policy analysis of CBME as it pertains to undergraduate medical education.

Accreditation

  • Cynthia Min

The Accreditation file is working on a scholarly analysis of Accreditation as it pertains to undergraduate medical education.

Policy Liaison

  • Sachin Pasricha
  • Naomi Reaka

The Policy Liaison file communicates closely with the CFMS Director of Government Affairs, Committee on Health Policy, and National Officer of Health Policy.

Interprofessional Education

  • Nikola Unic
  • Shirley Deng

The Interprofessional (IPE) file assists with CFMS interprofessional initiatives such as the student led clinic guide and the new national alliance of health professional student societies.

Unmatched Canadian Medical Graduates (uCMG)

  • Avrilynn Ding
  • Ailish Valeriano

The Unmatched Canadian Medical Graduates (uCMG) file works on aspects of CFMS activities that relate to unmatched students, and to create policy and CFMS initiatives that support students going through The Match. 

Research Committee

  • Jenny Ma
  • Emily Mardian

The Research Committee is a part of the CFMS Research Team along with the National Officers of Research. This file supports research initiatives of various CFMS activities within the Education Portfolio and beyond. The role of the Research Committee includes but is not limited to: helping with survey design, REB approvals, data analysis, and building the research aspect of the CFMS.

Ex Officio

Many CFMS members sit as ex officio members of the Education Committee. The role of the ex officio members is to update their organizations on Education Committee activities. If you have concerns, ideas or projects you’d like to share please contact both the Director of Education and the pertinent ex officio member
