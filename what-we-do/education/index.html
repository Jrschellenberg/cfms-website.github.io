Education

The Education Committee is a new committee under the Canadian Federation of Medical Students (CFMS) that aims to connect medical student societies and their respective students with the goal of enhancing medical education across the nation.

The CFMS Education portfolio is composed of the Vice-President Education, the CFMS Education Committee, and the VP Academic Roundtable.

What do we do?

  1. Education Committee: The Education Committee  was created in 2013 to work on improving undergraduate medical education across the nation.

  2. External Representation: The VP Ed and ex officio members of the Education Committee represent the CFMS to many external organizations.

  3. Resources: Please see our positions papers,  the Residency Matchbook , Student Run Clinics Toolkit , and our other resources for information
