  1. Call for Articles: on mental health of (future) health professionals

The European Sting is a political newspaper covering almost any political, economical and social topic. Specifically, for this call, they are seeking accepting submissions from medical students on the following topic: Interviews with ourselves - the mental health of future physicians. 

If you are interested in the topic above and you would like to share your ideas with us and the world, please send a full article to rdeurope@ifmsa.org no later than by the 30 of September.

The incoming articles need to adhere to the following specifications:

a)    Clearly defined brief title
b)    Up to 500 words article body text
c)    Profile picture of the writer
d)    100 words brief resume of the writer
e)    email address of the writer

Please send us your article as an attachment to your letter, and not as the actual letter. Also bear in mind that articles must be in a format that allows for them to be edited using a word processor. Hand-written and/or scanned articles will not be accepted.

Deadline: September 30th, 2017

 

  1. MAID – Foundational Online Module

Having a good grasp of the ethical issues surrounding Medical Assistance in Dying (MAiD) and a clear understanding of your own stance on the topic will help you have informed, objective and empathetic conversations with your patients.

Joule and the Canadian Medical Association (CMA) have created an unbiased educational offering specifically designed for Canadian physicians. This 90 minute foundation online module is offered to members of the CMA for free and covers the topic of Medical Assistance in Dying (MAID). Find more information here.
