Advocacy tracker

The purpose of the CFMS Advocacy Tracker is to summarize all CFMS position papers, advocacy status, and contact information. The CFMS Advocacy Tracker is maintained and updated by the National Officer of Health Policy (NOHP), the Committee on Health Policy (COHP), and Vice President Government Affairs. The NOHP and COHP will continue to ensure that the Advocacy Tracker is utilised and updated, so that the general membership may refer to the Advocacy Tracker to understand current CFMS advocacy efforts.
