What is the Spring General Meeting?

The Spring General Meeting (SGM) is an opportunity for Canadian medical students to get together and discuss important issues pertaining to medical education, advocacy, global health, wellness, and more. Join the conversation, get involved — we want to hear from you!

Travel Funding

Never been to a CFMS meeting? Interested in getting involved? Get your registration and travel costs covered by the CFMS by applying for Travel Funding! Each year the CFMS and MD Financial Management allocates funds to bring medical students to our annual meetings. It's a great way to learn more about what the CFMS does, get involved, and meet other medical students from across the country.

Call for Nominations: CFMS President-Elect

The election will take place at the CFMS Spring General Meeting (SGM) on Saturday April 28, 2018 in Halifax, NS. Votes will be cast by medical student society representatives and members of the Federation’s executive, as detailed in the organization’s bylaws.
