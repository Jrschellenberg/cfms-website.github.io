Medical Officer Training Program for Unmatched Students - MOTP Surge

 

In 2018, the CFMS in collaboration with the Canadian Armed Forces worked to create supernumerary Family Medicine positions through the Medical Officer Training Program (MOTP). This initiative is called MOTP Surge.

Eligibility criteria are as follows:

  • be a Canadian Citizen; and
  • be between 17 and 47 years old.

The CFMS is currently in conversation with the Canadian Armed Forces to determine if MOTP Surge will be offered for the 2019 match cycle. This webpage will be updated with any new developments. Please stay tuned.
