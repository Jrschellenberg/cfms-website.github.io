Communications

The Communications Team coordinates the internal and external communications of the CFMS, including this website; Facebook, Twitter and Instagram @CFMSFEMC profiles; and the CFMS Communiqué email newsletter. These mediums are used to promote the activities of the CFMS, as well as the successes and advocacy efforts of medical students across Canada. Connecting students at medical schools from across Canada, sharing ideas that are successful at one school that could be helpful to students at another, and encouraging grassroots engagement of CFMS members are core goals of the Communications Team.  Further to that goal, the Communications Team coordinates the CFMS Representatives/VP Externals Round Table from each member medical school.

For media requests, please contact Christina Schweitzer, CFMS VP Communications, at vpcommunications@cfms.org.
