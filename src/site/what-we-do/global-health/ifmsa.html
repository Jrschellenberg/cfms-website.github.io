1.  IFMSA Executive Board Call for Applicants

As a member of the CFMS, you are eligible to become an executive of the IFMSA. Candidatures can be submitted for the following Executive Board positions for the term 2016/17.

1         President

2         Vice-President for Activities, serving also as Secretary General

3         Vice-President for Finance, serving also as Treasurer

4         Vice-President for Members

5         Vice-President for External Affairs

6         Vice-President for Capacity Building

7         Vice-President for Public Relations and Communication

The task descriptions for these Executive Board positions can be found in the Bylaws, paragraph 5.8-14. Please note that by applying for an Executive Board position, you are committing yourself to carry out the tasks specified in the Bylaws. A more comprehensive description of the tasks of each EB member can be found here: goo.gl/RQxTnl. Candidatures for Executive Board positions should be sent to gs@ifmsa.org with the code [CAND EB] in the beginning of the subject of your email, followed by the name of the position you apply for.

For support, contact eb@ifmsa.org. Please note that the EB will not provide assistance concerning the content of the application, but solely on the requirements set by Bylaws.

Deadline: February 1st, 2017 at 23:59 GMT to gs@ifmsa.org

Please send each type of application in a separate email and put the relevant [code between brackets] for the type of application in the Subject of the email.

Questions? Contact: eb@ifmsa.org. 

 

2. SRT 2017: Facing Today's Global Challenges in Health Care

It is with great pleasure that we open the call for participants for IFMSA-Québec's 2017 edition of the Sub Regional Training (SRT) in the charming Québec City! The training will take place from February 17 to Feb. 19, 2017.

The “SRT 2017: Facing Today's Global Challenges in Health Care” will feature two parallel streams answering the needs of today’s medical students: the first one will feature sessions on what is needed to be a good leader and to translate one’s audacious ideas and projects into impactful actions; the second one will be oriented toward Healthcare in Danger: raising awareness on the current issues of violence against healthcare professionals, patients and institutions. Some joint sessions will allow all participants to meet and get trained as a group.

See the proposed agenda and more info - Click here. 

You can apply via the following link: http://bit.ly/2hB3dn6.

Deadline to apply is January 25, 2017. 

Participants can expect to pay a fee of 35$CAD which includes food and accommodation. Questions? Contact projets@ifmsa.qc.ca.

 

3.  ADAM Summer School – focus on anesthesiology and acute medicine

The ADAM Summer School is a medical summer school, organized for medical students from all around the world. During the week we will combine an interesting educational experience with a fun social program. The medical programme of the ADAM Summer School focuses on anesthesiology and acute medicine. You will attain practical skills through simulation, for which the practical sessions take place at the Simulation Center of the VU University medical center in Amsterdam. Not only will you be simulating, there will also be opportunities to develop other skills, such as performing intubations and coniotomies. Furthermore, you will visit inspiring lectures and workshops.   

Ever wanted to have lunch in the Vondelpark? Or make a city tour through Amsterdam? During the social programme, you will explore the beautiful capital Amsterdam! There is much to discover with the rich history, culture and nightlife that Amsterdam has to offer! Also, you will have plenty of opportunities to connect with your peers from all over the world.

Course fee: International students: 575 euros. Included: housing, breakfast, lunch for 5 days and dinner for 6 days, airport pick-up service, medical- & social programme.

You can apply through the following link: https://goo.gl/forms/zyd4A4IM0Wmj8vi23

Deadline for registration: January 31st, 2017 23:59 CET

4. European Sting newspaper submission opportunity – topic: WHO and Young Doctors – place for improvement?

The European Sting is a political newspaper that aims to bring unbiased and trustworthy information to their readers not only about European Affairs, but about almost any political, economical and social topic. Furthermore, the Sting welcomes its readers to take part in this constructive and critical dialogue! As per the IFMSA's partnership with the Sting, any medical student worldwide is able to voice their ideas, concerns, opinions and dreams in a global online magazine. During this month, we will be accepting articles on the following topic: WHO and young doctors – place for improvement?

If you are interested in the topic above and you would like to share your ideas with us and the world, please send a full article to rdeurope@ifmsa.org by no later than the 31st of January.

The incoming articles need to adhere to the following specifications:

a)    Clearly defined brief title
b)    Up to 500 words article body text
c)    Profile picture of the writer
d)    100 words brief resume of the writer
e)    email address of the writer

Please send your articles in the form of a Word file or PDF!

 

5. 23rd World Association for Sexual Health Congress – call to join IFMSA delegation 

It’s with great pleasure that I am opening the call for the IFMSA Delegation that will participate in the World Association for Sexual Health (WAS) Congress – an opportunity for medical students to learn about sexology, sexual pleasure and sexual health and rights in academic/scientific setting. Opportunity includes free registration, a workshop on Sexual Health and Sexual Pleasure specifically aimed at medical students, and a spot in a spot in a plenary panel on sexual pleasure.

23rd World Association for Sexual Health Congress

When: May 28 to 31, 2017

Where: Prague (Czech Republic)

What: The World Association for Sexual Health (WAS) is an international umbrella organization representing sexological societies and sexologists worldwide. Founded in 1978 in Rome, Italy, the WAS main goal is to promote sexual health for all through sexological science. They hold biannual congresses, being the upcoming one (23rd) in Prague. More Information: http://www.was2017.org/

General Remarks:

· Delegates are requested to actively participate in the delegation's preparation process for the meeting and contribute to working on IFMSA’s strategy for this meeting.

· Unfortunately, IFMSA won't be able to financially support neither travel nor accommodation for the meeting. We will issue letters of support that will help you in your fundraising efforts, if needed.

Selection Criteria:

· Experience in IFMSA (includes CFMS/local school experiences).

· Experience in the topic of the meeting and work done at local, national and international levels.

· Potential individual empowerment and expected outcomes at a local, national or international level.

· Regional representation and Gender balance will also be taken into account.

Call for input: If you are interested in providing input regarding the activity of IFMSA before and during WAS 2017, please provide it through the input for external meetings form. There will be more information sent via IFMSA communication servers prior to the meeting.

How can you apply?

· Fill in the Application Form

· Candidature Form signed and stamped by the NMO President which needs to be sent to Lra@ifmsa.org with [WAS-APPL] and the name of the applicant in the email subject.

· Both things need to be completed/sent before February 12 23:59 GMT

Questions/concerns? lra@ifmsa.org.
