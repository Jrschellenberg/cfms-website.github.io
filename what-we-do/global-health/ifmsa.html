 1. IFMSA Executive Board Call for Applicants:

As a member of the CFMS, you are eligible to become an executive of the IFMSA. Candidatures can be submitted for the following Executive Board positions for the term 2016/17.

1         President

2         Vice-President for Activities, serving also as Secretary General

3         Vice-President for Finance, serving also as Treasurer

4         Vice-President for Members

5         Vice-President for External Affairs

6         Vice-President for Capacity Building

7         Vice-President for Public Relations and Communication

 The task descriptions for these Executive Board positions can be found in the Bylaws, paragraph 5.8-14. Please note that by applying for an Executive Board position, you are committing yourself to carry out the tasks specified in the Bylaws. A more comprehensive description of the tasks of each EB member can be found here: goo.gl/RQxTnl.

Candidates for any IFMSA Officials Position have to submit the following documents:

·       A filled out candidature form, signed and stamped by the President of the candidate’s National Member Organisation. If the candidate is the NMO President the confirmation must be signed by a suitable alternative NMO board member. The Candidature Form is attached as GS_2017MM_CF_IFMSA EB_[name of EB position]_[your name]. Change “[name of EB position]” to the position you are applying for and “[your name]” to your first and last name.

·       A filled out Technical Data Card for Officials. The Technical Data Card is attached as GS_2017MM_TDC_IFMSA EB_[name of EB position]_[your name]. Change “[name of EB position]” to the position you are applying for and “[your name]” to your first and last name.

·       A motivation letter and a plan of action.

·       A curriculum vitae. Proof of information provided in the CV should be available upon request by a National Member Organisation

·       One copy of the identity page of the candidate’s passport for registration purposes.


Candidatures for Executive Board positions should be sent to gs@ifmsa.org with the code [CAND EB] in the beginning of the subject of your email, followed by the name of the position you apply for.

For support, contact eb@ifmsa.org. Please note that the EB will not provide assistance concerning the content of the application, but solely on the requirements set by Bylaws.

 All applications should be sent maximum by the 1st of February 2017, 23:59 GMT to gs@ifmsa.org. Please send each type of application in a separate email and put the relevant [code between brackets]for the type of application in the Subject of the email.

Questions? Contact: eb@ifmsa.org.

  

2. SRT 2017: Facing Today's Global Challenges in Health Care

It is with great pleasure that we open the call for participants for IFMSA-Québec's 2017 edition of the Sub Regional Training (SRT) in the charming Québec City! The training will take place from February 17 to Feb. 19, 2017.

The “SRT 2017: Facing Today's Global Challenges in Health Care” will feature two parallel streams answering the needs of today’s medical students: the first one will feature sessions on what is needed to be a good leader and to translate one’s audacious ideas and projects into impactful actions; the second one will be oriented toward Healthcare in Danger: raising awareness on the current issues of violence against healthcare professionals, patients and institutions. Some joint sessions will allow all participants to meet and get trained as a group.

See the proposed agenda and more info - Click here. 

You can apply via the following link: http://bit.ly/2hB3dn6.

 Deadline to apply is January 25, 2017. 

Participants can expect to pay a fee of 35$CAD which includes food and accommodation.

 Questions? Contact projets@ifmsa.qc.ca.
