  1. "Climate Change and Health Podcast Series:

The Global Health Advocates are excited to launch an 8-part podcast series featuring local experts discussing various facets of the interactions between climate change and health. Please visit our Soundcloud account to listen to each episode:

https://soundcloud.com/cfmsglobalhealth

**Please, note that the Canadian Federation of Medical Students and its affiliates do not necessarily endorse the views expressed in these interviews." 

 

  1. One Young World Scholarship Opportunity

This year the Johnson & Johnson Global Community Impact Group is collaborating with One Young World to offer scholarships to eight inspiring young leaders to attend the One Young World Summit 2017 in Bogotá, Colombia.

https://www.oneyoungworld.com/johnson-johnson-one-young-world-scholarship-program

Deadline: June 19, 2017

 

  1. Unite for Sight

The CFMS has partnered with Unite for Sight’s Global Health University to offer members discounted rates for the certificate programs – at a reduced rate of $75.00. Members must use their university email addresses when they register. Once registered, you will receive a link to the materials by email. 

https://maestro.technolutions.net/uniteforsight/cfms

 

  1. Joule 3D printing innovation

Joule, a subsidiary company of the CMA, currently has a competition for 3D printing innovative ideas!

"We're asking for your imagination to create 3D printed product innovations that aim to remove barriers to quality of life. Winning solutions will focus on affordability, accessibility and convenience."

https://www.cma.ca/En/Pages/joule-innovation-challenge-1.aspx

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q2ZroeKGm-o

Deadline: June 19, 2017

 

  1. CFMS General Meeting resolutions feedback

For the past 6 months, the CFMS has been evaluating a more effective way of managing position papers and resolutions, including their submission and adoption. Since SGM, this issue is now of high priority. A small working group consisting of medical society presidents, CFMS representatives, National Officers, Global Health Liaisons and Government Affairs & Advocacy Committee representatives has been created with aim of submitting a final set of recommendations at the 2017 CFMS AGM. We are asking for feedback from the general CFMS membership via way of the following Google Form: https://goo.gl/forms/gapaPXhruVw14t3A2. Please contact CFMS Vice-President of Government Affairs, Sarah Silverberg, at vpgovtaffairs@cfms.org about any questions or concerns. The feedback form will be available until June 30, 2017.
