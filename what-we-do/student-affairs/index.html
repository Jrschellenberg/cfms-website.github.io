Student Affairs

Welcome to the Student Affairs Portfolio of the CFMS! We are involved in member services, including our discounts, leadership awards, and web-based services. We also play an integral part in promoting student wellness, and connecting medical students across the country.

Visit our NEW Wellness Site!

  • Visit our newly developed CFMS Wellness Site for nation-wide wellness tips for each medical school!

What do we do?

  1. Wellness: Resources on how you can access student wellness resources at your school, and in your surrounding community.
  2. Leadership Awards: The CFMS, in collaboration with MD Financial Management, have created an award designed to recognize some of the passionate, dedicated and caring medical student leaders across Canada.
  3. Student Discounts: The CFMS offers discounts for many services, including UpToDate, textbooks, and even LASIK MD Laser eye surgery!
