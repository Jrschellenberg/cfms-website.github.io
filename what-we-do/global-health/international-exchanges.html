IFMSA-CFMS International Exchange Program

Applications for summer 2017 exchanges are now open! Deadline to apply is Oct 21, 2016 @ 11:59pm EST.

CFMS participates in an international exchange program where medical students undertake clinical or research projects, each four-weeks in duration, in a foreign country or region. The exchange program is run independently by each host country or region. It is for students who are independent, self-directed, open-minded, and able to handle unforeseen challenges and take learning into his or her own hands.

This past season (Summer 2016) over 160 students participated in exchange. In the upcoming season, a total of 167 placement spots are available in over 40 countries! 

For more information about the exchange program and how to apply, please refer to the follow document.
