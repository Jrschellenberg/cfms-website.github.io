  1. SCOPH International Summer School 2017

It is with great pleasure that MEDSA-RWANDA is delighted to invite all medical students around the globe to the country of a thousand hills, RWANDA, for this year’s SCOPH International Summer School 2017 under theme “Non Communicable Diseases & Climate Change and Health”. The SISS2017 will take place from the 10th to 26th August 2017 in Kigali, Rwanda.

The International Summer School is a 2-3 weeks MEDSAR-Rwanda Summer program that will gather in one place medical students from different health systems and different health challenge backgrounds to share experience, learn and discus the current biggest Public Health issues.

For detailed program visit: http://www.siss.medsarwanda.org/training/

Applications are now open, apply now and fill this application form

For more information here is our website: siss.medsarwanda.org

Registration deadline: 15th May 2017 - 23:59 GMT

Questions? email siss@medsarwanda.org

 

  1. Rethinking Justice, our 7th Symposium on HIV, Law and Human Rights

You’re invited to join us for the day on Thursday, June 15, 2017, for Rethinking Justice, our 7th Symposium on HIV, Law and Human Rights.

This year’s Symposium is devoted to a critical look at the unjust criminalization of HIV non-disclosure. Themes to be discussed include:

  • Current state of criminalization: Canada and the world 
  • Voices rising: Speaking out about the experience of HIV criminalization
  • Science of HIV transmission: recent applications, emerging issues
  • Advocacy updates and ways forward

The full-day event will take place at the Chelsea Hotel, 33 Gerrard St. W., in Toronto. We will release a list of speakers and topics for this year’s Symposium shortly.

Please save the date for this exciting and informative event on June 15. Keep an eye on our webpage at aidslaw.ca/symposium for more information, including a list of speakers.

You can register to attend the Symposium today. Visit our registration page to confirm your spot.

For more information, please contact info@aidslaw.ca.
