Why create a CFMS account?

As a medical student in one of the member schools in the CFMS, you are automatically eligible to create a CFMS account. By creating an account, you get access to an extensive list of member services and resources. Benefits include:

Student Discounts

Exclusive discounts are available for Hotels, Textbooks, UpToDate©, Disability Insurance, and even LASIK MD Laser Vision Correction. For a full list of discounts, click here.

Access to CFMS Electives and Interview Databases

The CFMS maintains anonymous databases of external elective reviews and residency interview reviews. Great for planning electives or preparing for your CaRMS interviews.

Exclusive Access to Awards & Grants

As a member of the CFMS, you are eligible to apply for the annual CFMS - MD Financial Management Leadership Awards. One award is given each year to a student from each member school. To find out more, click here.

The CFMS also issues grants to fund medical student-initiated projects around the country, with a maximum funding amount of $3000.

Access to the CFMS Match Book

The Match Book is a joint initiative of the CFMS and CaRMS designed to educate medical students about the Match and assist with career planning. Read more about it here.

Connect with other CFMS Members and Alumni

Attend one of our bi-annual CFMS meetings, and meet your colleagues from across the country!
